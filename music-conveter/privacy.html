Privacy Policy

App: MP3 Converter

Effective Date: July 19, 2026

Contact: mduzzalmia.dev@gmail.com

This Privacy Policy explains how MP3 Converter ("the App," "we," "us") handles information when you use the App. We built the App to perform video and audio conversion directly on your device and have designed it to collect as little personal information as possible.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, and the App does not require you to create an account, log in, or provide your name, email address, or any other personal identifier to use its core features.

The App does not access your contacts, location, microphone, or camera.

2. Information We Access On Your Device

To provide the App's features, we request the following permissions directly from iOS. This access occurs only on your device, and your files are never uploaded to us or to any third party.

  • Photo & Video Library (Read Access)
    Used so you can select videos from your library to convert into audio. All selected files are processed locally on your device.
  • Photo Library (Add Access)
    Used only when you choose to save a converted audio or video file back to your Photos library.

You can manage these permissions anytime in: Settings → Privacy & Security → Photos.

3. Files You Convert

All audio and video conversion happens locally on your device using Apple's AVFoundation framework.

We never upload your media files to any server and do not retain copies of your files outside your device's local storage, including any in-app history or file list.

4. Subscriptions and Payment Information

The App offers optional auto-renewing subscriptions (weekly, monthly, and yearly) for premium features.

All purchases are processed entirely by Apple's App Store / StoreKit. We never receive or store your payment card information, Apple ID password, or billing details.

To manage and validate subscription entitlements, we use RevenueCat, a third-party subscription management platform. RevenueCat receives:

  • An anonymous app-generated user identifier.
  • Transaction and App Store receipt information.

RevenueCat does not receive your name, email address, or payment details.

Learn more: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

5. Analytics and Advertising

The App does not include third-party advertising SDKs and does not display ads.

Aside from the subscription validation described above, we do not use analytics or tracking SDKs that identify you individually, nor do we track you across other apps or websites for advertising purposes.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

The only third party involved in the operation of the App is RevenueCat, which is used solely to validate subscriptions and purchases.

We may also disclose information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us and we will promptly address the matter.

8. Data Retention

Converted files and app preferences remain stored locally on your device. You control their retention by deleting files or uninstalling the App.

Subscription validation records maintained by RevenueCat are retained according to RevenueCat's own retention policies.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal information we hold about you.

Because the App is designed to minimize personal data collection, most privacy requests can be managed through iOS Settings or by contacting RevenueCat directly.

You may also contact us using the email address below regarding any privacy request.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. If we make material changes, we will update the Effective Date above and, where appropriate, notify you within the App.

Your continued use of the App after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
